Goal

As mentioned our introduction, we're not just trying to solve one side, but rather an entire layer. Take note that the side pieces don't just match the white side, but also the red and blue centers as well.

The Technique

This move is almost excactly the same as the one we used in the step before for the corners. However, we're going to be doing it twice (once each direction with a small rotation inbetween).

Starting Position

The T

This move will take a piece from the bottom layer and move it into position in the top layer without offseting any other corner pieces that might already be in position.

The Moves

With your T in place:

  1. Rotate the bottom layer away from where it needs to go
  2. Bring that side down
  3. Bring the bottom layer back over
  4. Rotate that side back up

(Turn the entire cube so that the side is now facing you and repeat steps 1-4 but the other way.)

  1. Rotate the bottom layer the other direction than before
  2. Bring the other side down
  3. Bring the bottom layer back over
  4. Rotate the side back up
