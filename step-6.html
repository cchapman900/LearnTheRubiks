Goal

The Cross

As mentioned our introduction, we're not just trying to solve one side, but rather an entire layer. Take note that the side pieces don't just match the white side, but also the red and blue centers as well.

The Technique

This technique will switch the positions of 3 third layer side pieces. As with the 3-side rotation, it takes a little bit of planning to be able to know which pieces to rotate. A good starting point is to find a piece that is already in position and use it as your anchor point.

<illustration of the move (arrow from middle piece)>

Starting Position

With the 3 pieces you are planning to move on the top layer, and on the left, front and right positions, rotate the cube towards you 180 degrees so those pieces are on the bottom layer, and furthest from you.

Top pieces in the front and right, then...


...flip the cube upside-down towards you, with those pieces in the back

The Moves

  1. Middle verticle section down
  2. Bottom to the left
  3. Middle section back up
  4. Bottom 180 degrees around
  5. Middle section back down
  6. Bottom to the left
  7. Middle section back up
