Goal

With our sides solved from the previous layer, we will complete the rest of the first layer with the corners.

The Technique

This move is almost excactly the same as the one we used in the step before for the corners. However, it is just one layer up.

<illustration of the move (arrow from middle piece)>

Starting Position

Before going into the move, we will need to make sure that the target piece we are trying to solve is on the bottom layer facing out towards us.

Starting on the bottom right...


...or on the bottom left:

The Moves

With the 1st layer facing up, and the target piece on the bottom layer facing out towards you:

  1. Rotate the bottom layer across from the side it's on
  2. Bring the side it was on down
  3. Bring the bottom layer back over
  4. Rotate that side back up
