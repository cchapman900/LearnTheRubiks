Goal

The Cross

As mentioned our introduction, we're not just trying to solve one side, but rather an entire layer. Take note that the side pieces don't just match the white side, but also the red and blue centers as well.

The Technique

While the concept of this idea is a little harder than the last move, it is very similar in the steps themselves, so it should be much easier than starting from scratch.

This technique will rotate 3 corner pieces at once. Figuring out which ones to rotate is the hardest part of this step. The ideal scenario is to have one corner in the correct rotation.

<illustration of the move (arrow from middle piece)>

Starting Position

With the piece that won't move in the back left side, rotate the cube 180 degrees towards you so that that piece is now on the bottom front left.

<correct starting position, and incorrect starting positions>

The Moves

With the...:

  1. Right side down
  2. Bottom to the left
  3. Right side back up
  4. Bottom side to the left
  5. Bottom back down
  6. Bottom rotated 180 degrees around
  7. Right side back up
  8. Bottom another 180 degrees around
